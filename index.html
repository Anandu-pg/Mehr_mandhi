Our Story

A Legacy of Authentic Arabian Flavours

Nestled in the heart of Cherthala, Mehr Mandi & Grills was born from a deep passion for authentic Arabian and Persian culinary traditions. Our chefs bring decades of expertise, crafting each dish the traditional way — slow-cooking tender meats over fragrant wood fires, layering saffron-kissed rice with time-honoured spice blends.

We believe dining is an experience. From the moment you step into our warm, beautifully designed space to the last bite of our signature Mandi, every detail is crafted to make you feel like royalty. Mehr — meaning Love & Generosity in Persian — captures the very essence of what we do.
